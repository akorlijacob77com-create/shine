Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Here's everything you need to know about joining, events, and life at Shine Society.

Who can join Shine Society?

Shine Society is open to young people aged 13–35 who are passionate about friendship, fun, personal growth, and making a positive impact. No experience needed — just bring your energy and good vibes!

Is there any membership fee?

No! Membership is completely free. We believe everyone should have the chance to shine without financial barriers. Some events may have small optional contributions (e.g. for transport or snacks), but nothing is mandatory.

How often do you hold events and meetings?

We usually have at least 2–3 activities per month — mix of social hangouts, charity projects, workshops, and celebration nights. Big events (like our annual outreach) happen 1–2 times a year. We announce everything on WhatsApp, Instagram, and our website.

What kind of activities do you do?

We do a bit of everything: charity drives, beach clean-ups, skill-building workshops (leadership, public speaking, creative arts), game nights, themed parties, deep talks, and community impact projects. Check our Activities and Gallery sections for real examples!

Do I need any special skills or experience to join?

No special skills required! Whether you're outgoing or shy, creative or analytical — there's a place for you. We believe everyone has something unique to bring, and we help each other grow.

How do I stay updated on events and news?

Join our WhatsApp group (link shared after registration), follow us on Instagram and TikTok, or check the Events and Blog sections on the website. We also send emails for important updates.

Can I volunteer or help organize events?

Absolutely! Once you're a member, you can join committees, suggest ideas, or even lead small projects. We love when members take initiative — it's how we grow stronger together.

What happens after I submit the registration form?

We'll review your application within 3–5 days and contact you via WhatsApp or email to welcome you, add you to our group, and invite you to your first event or orientation. Welcome aboard!
