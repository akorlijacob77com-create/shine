Photo Upload Note – For Admins Only

To keep the gallery fresh and reflective of our latest activities:

  • Upload high-quality, well-lit photos (minimum 1200px wide recommended)
  • Always resize images before upload (aim for 200–400 KB per photo to keep page fast)
  • Use descriptive alt text for accessibility (e.g. "Shine Society members planting trees during 2025 outreach")
  • Group photos by category when adding (charity, social, workshops, celebration)
  • Remove any photo that includes minors without parental consent
  • Current method: Replace placeholder Unsplash links with real file paths (e.g. images/events/2025-cleanup-01.jpg)
  • Future improvement: Add admin dashboard for drag-and-drop uploads + auto-categorization
